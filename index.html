Armament

The Strv 122 is equipped with a 120mm L/44 smoothbore gun, capable of firing a variety of ammunition, including APFSDS, HEAT, and MPAT rounds.

It also features a coaxial 7.62mm machine gun and a roof-mounted 7.62mm anti-aircraft machine gun.

Technology

The Strv 122 incorporates advanced technology to enhance its combat capabilities and survivability on the battlefield.

Its modular composite armor provides protection against various threats, including kinetic energy penetrators and shaped charges.

The tank's fire control system includes thermal imaging, laser rangefinder, and ballistic computer for accurate target acquisition and engagement.

Furthermore, it is equipped with an NBC protection system and smoke grenade launchers for battlefield survivability.

The Strv 122 is powered by a MTU MB 873 Ka-501 turbocharged diesel engine, delivering 1,500 horsepower.

With this powerplant, the tank can achieve a top speed of 70 km/h on roads and has a range of over 500 kilometers.

Its advanced suspension system allows for excellent off-road performance, enabling the tank to traverse rough terrain with ease.

The Strv 122 can climb gradients of up to 60% and cross trenches up to 3 meters wide.
